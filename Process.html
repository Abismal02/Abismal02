Process Paper

Originally, my group picked the battle of Stalingrad as our topic, however, after some
procrastination and boredom, one group member came across a video on the fall of The
Berlin Wall. This video really caught their attention because they suggested that we switch
our topic to the fall of The Berlin Wall. After some more in-depth research, everyone agreed
to switch to that instead, it had all the requirements that it needed to be a turning point and,
as an added bonus, was significantly more interesting than the previously proposed topic.
The video that sparked this idea became the basis of our project, it almost outlined what we

prior knowledge and experience with making websites. The argument that we were
defending was that the fall of The Berlin Wall was a major turning point in global and
German history, as it marked the end of the Cold War and not only started the reunification
of Germany but also left it very much divided. The fall of The Berlin Wall is significant in
history because it led to the end of the Cold War, a war famous for not having any real
battles, and that can be credited to the fall of The Berlin Wall for ending it before it
escalated to such.
